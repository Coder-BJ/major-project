Project Information

In this project, we developed a Multimodal health analyzer with a primary focus on analyzing X-ray images and providing disease predictions based on both visual data and manually input symptoms. The project utilized machine learning algorithms to train the system on X-ray datasets, enabling accurate detection of specific diseases. Additionally, a user-friendly interface was developed using Flask for easy interaction. Research Papers

Our system is built upon extensive research in the fields of medical image analysis, machine learning, and multimodal health diagnostics. The following papers have been referenced and contributed to our project:

1. Medical Imaging with Deep Learning: A comprehensive review of X-ray analysis techniques and disease prediction models.
2. Multimodal Disease Diagnosis: Combining manual symptom input with image-based diagnostics for enhanced accuracy.
3. Machine Learning in Healthcare: A guide to applying machine learning techniques for predictive health analysis.
4. Advanced Medical Imaging: Exploration of MRI and CT scan integration into diagnostic tools.
